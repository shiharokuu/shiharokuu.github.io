仰々しい(ぎょうぎょうしい)

pompous ★★☆☆☆

someone who is too formal, pompous, high-falutin'. You're like "Hi, dude!" and they're like "Salutations, my good man! Verily the sun doth shine like the Emperor's benign gaze upon this blessed land!"

仰 (pompous / look up to) + 天 (heaven) = 仰天 (flabbergasted )

to be flabbergasted - in a good way OR a bad way.
