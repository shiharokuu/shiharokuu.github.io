察 police

(crown) + 祭 (festival)
★★★★☆
14 strokes
ILL PAIR

Onyomi

SATSU

Mnemonic

I SAW TWO police at the festival, wearing crowns.

Kunyomi

さっ*する CULTURAL CODE WORD. To infer the feelings of other people (usually in the sense of being a good host or sentitive friend) (as opposed to 気が利く , which refers to actually physically doing something for that person, based on what you さっするed!). CCW
★★★☆☆

Jukugo

警察(けいさつ)

police ★★★★★ NP
警 (the fuzz) + 察 (police) = 警察 (police)

推察(すいさつ)

guess or speculate ★★★☆☆
推 (infer) + 察 (police) = 推察 (guess or speculate )

to guess or speculate about the meaning of someone's behavior or words. 推察 is only used when you have no real proof.
