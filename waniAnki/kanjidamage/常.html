常 usual

尚 (all the more) + 市 (small city/dagger　radical)
★★★★★
11 strokes

Onyomi

JOU

Mnemonic

It's usual for communists to stab you with a dagger, all the more if the communist happens to be . . . JOE Stalin!

Kunyomi

つね ( に ) 2 meanings;

1-ordinarily, normally that is the case

2 - I do this all the time!
★★★★★

Jukugo

日常(にちじょう)

everyday life ★★★★☆
日 (sun, day) + 常 (usual) = 日常 (everyday life)

everyday life - you know, on the quotidian tip.
常識(じょうしき)

common sense ★★★★☆
常 (usual) + 識 (be conscious) = 常識 (common sense)

非常(ひじょう)

emergency ★★★★☆
非 (injustice / mistake) + 常 (usual) = 非常 (emergency)

emergency. This is the word on all the emergency exits and emergency firehoses. But you'd never say "I'm having an 非常！”
非常(ひじょう) にxxx

exceedingly xxx ★★★☆☆
非 (injustice / mistake) + 常 (usual) = 非常 (exceedingly xxx)

in an emergency? No! That would be too simple. ひじょうに means, exceedingly or extremely.
