Jukugo

触角(しょっかく)

antenna ★☆☆☆☆
触 (touch) + 角 (horn/corner) = 触角 (antenna)

an antenna or feeler, like an insect or lobster's got
肌触り(はだざわり)

texture ★☆☆☆☆
肌 (human skin) + 触 (touch) = 肌触り (texture)

the texture of something (i.e. how it feels when rubbed on your skin)
感触(かんしょく)

touch-sensation ☆☆☆☆☆
感 (feeling) + 触 (touch) = 感触 (touch-sensation)

a physical sensation of touch, as in "I've lost all feeling in my left ball." Also can be used as "impression", as in, "What kind of feeling did you get from the interview? Do you think it went well?"
