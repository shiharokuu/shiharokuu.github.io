惑 be misguided or tempted

(spear) + 口 (mouth/small box radical) + (bottomheart)
★★★☆☆
12 strokes

Onyomi

WAKU

Mnemonic

The devil on my shoulder used his mouth to tempt me to stab with a spear, the girl who WALKED on my heart.

Kunyomi

まど*う get bewildered (by your own ignorance, accidentally) or led astray (by someone else, deliberately)
★★☆☆☆

Jukugo

迷惑(めいわく)

annoyance ★★★☆☆
迷 (perplexed) + 惑 (be misguided or tempted) = 迷惑 (annoyance)

annoyance. Can be used as an adjective (迷惑なｘｘｘ） or as a verb (迷惑をかける＝to annoy).
誘惑(ゆうわく) する

tempt ★★★☆☆
誘 (invite / entice) + 惑 (be misguided or tempted) = 誘惑 (tempt )
