Jukugo

反対(はんたい) する

against it!!!!!!!!!! ★★★★☆
反 (anti) + 対 (against) = 反対 (against it!!!!!!!!!!)

to be against something
反則(はんそく)

foul! To break a rule! ★★☆☆☆
反 (anti) + 則 (rule) = 反則 (foul! To break a rule!)

反抗(はんこう) する

rebel ★★☆☆☆
反 (anti) + 抗 (oppose) = 反抗 (rebel)

to be rebellious, defiant. To start a revolt. Unlike the related words 抵抗　（ていこう）　and 対抗　（たいこう）、　反抗 has the nuance of 'rebel for the fuck of it,' like a teenager would.
反応(はんのう)

a reaction ★★☆☆☆ FP
反 (anti) + 応 (react) = 反応 (a reaction )

a reaction - despite the 反 kanji, 反応 is not neccesairly a bad reaction. As a noun, it's value-free. (careful! This is pronounced NOU, not OU)
