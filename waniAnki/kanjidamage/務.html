事務所(じむしょ)

office ★★★★☆ ABU
事 (action/ incident) + 務 (perform a task) + 所 (area / attribute) = 事務所 (office)

the office. Although, they say that some Yakuza groups also call themselves 'jimusho' as in, 'If you don't pay, they won't be happy down at the office.'
義務(ぎむ)

social obligation ★★★☆☆
義 (righteousness) + 務 (perform a task) = 義務 (social obligation)

an obligation to society - like voting, paying taxes, going to school, or in some cases, the army.
